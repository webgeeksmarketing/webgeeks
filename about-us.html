Locations

Common Challenges

01.

Uncertainty about Marketing Direction

For small to mid-sized organization, marfketing and business planning oftern fails to get the attention needed to guide the efforts to achieve the organizations goals. Brand Machine from Web Geeks is a time-tested, proprietary planning process that identifies all of the key aspects to produce both a brand guide and marketing plan as well as the analytical feedback to measure the impact of the marketing activities.

02.

Building an Effective Marketing Platform

Most organizations recognize the central role of a website in their marketing stack - but they often come up short in creating an engaging presentation of their brand. Web Geeks has the experience and capability to build an attractive, purpose driven website, social media profiles, business directory listings, email newsletter, video elements and more that will wow and drive action across your enitre marketing platform.

03.

Effectively Communicatiing Brand Story

Knowing what you want to say and actually saying it to the right people at the right time can be very challenging. Web Geeks working from a stratgic plan will develop customized marketing campaigns utilizing digital (PPC, SEO, Social, Video, Email, Content) and more traditional forms of advertising to drive engagement with your brand and compell the targeted audience to take action.

04.

Measuring Your Marketing Impact

Is your money spent effectively? Are the campaign activities producing results? Are the corporate goals and the marketing activities in sync? Without good data and actionable feedback, how can your organization maximize its marketing activies? Web Geeks closes the circle with marketing data for every activity and through reports, client meetings and on-going collaberation, we can get the best out of your marketing.

Our Experience

Digital technology and online marketing is often a mystery and stressful for a business owner to embrace. Our goal is to take the mystery out of it and eliminate the stress. We put our understanding and expertise to work for you. The story of Web Geeks Marketing started more than 20 years ago when we provided a local bicycle shop with a computerized point of sale system. Since that time, we have worked with hundreds of organizations across Canada, the USA and the Caribbean.

1990 - Retail POS & GST

Responding to the introduction of GST, we began by helping retailers implement computerized POS systems.​

1994 - Walmart & CRM

As a result of the Canadian invasion of Walmart, we introduced CRM and advanced inventory management to help retailers compete.

2000 - Amazon & E-Commerce

Responding to the early influence of Amazon, we began helping our clients establish a retail presence on the web.​

2001 - Online POS

Working with a small-cap public company, we developed the world’s first online POS system for specialty retail called RetailPort.

2004 - Planes, Printing & Pets

Having developed expertise taking brick and mortar operations online, we build portals for leasing aircraft, online printing and answering pet owners’ questions and product orders. ​

2007 - SEO & Churches

An early adopter of search engine optimization we drove traffic to our client sites. We also created one of the first web builders for churches.​

2009 - Relocation to Windsor

In response to growth of US client-base, we relocated our operations to Windsor, Canada allowing us to better serve internationally.​

2018 - Introduction of Brand Machine

The culmination of nearly 30 years, Brand Machine provides a framework for marketing success.​
