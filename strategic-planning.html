Brand Development

Brand Development is far more than coming with a new logo or a slogan. Web Geeks' customers understand that marketing success is entirely related to how people perceive and value their brand. When brand equity increases fortunes are made and the loss of brand favor can spell doom for the organization. Web Geeks is focused on our client's brand - identifying and helping organizations deliver on their brand promises, understanding and communicating their brand personality (within and outside of the organization), generating content messaging and creative consistent with the brand personality and in a way that engages the target audience and encouraging brand promotion by a satisfied constituency.

  • Engage the favor and support of your market.
  • Grow market share, visibility and preference.
  • Develop assurance of marketing success.

Marketing Analytics

Our clients want to know that their marketing investment is achieving the desired results which makes Marketing Analytics critical to judging the quality and effectiveness of your digital marketing program. One of the biggest advantages of Marketing Analytics to marketers is the wealth of detailed data available from every digital marketing tactic. Social media insights, AdWords interactions, website traffic and keyword positioning are examples of the rich data feedback that is available to inform the strategic decision making process.
Web Geeks tracks the many data points, consolidates the feedback and initiates how data informs our clients marketing initiatives. The result is better strategy and better marketing results.

  • Know what marketing activities are producing results.
  • Compare initiatives to find the best bang for the buck.
  • Identify trends to embrace or avoid for best advantage.
